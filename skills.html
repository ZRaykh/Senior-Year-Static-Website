My Skills as a Professional Photographer

One of the skills I'm most proud of is my photography. I've been paid before to take photos for people at events and made it far on photo contests. I prefer to take photos of nature or urban environments rather than people, but overtime people began paying me to take photos for them and that skill grew!

I use a Cannon Rebel T7 as my camera, but I am currently trying to upgrade to a new one. The right image is one of my photos.
